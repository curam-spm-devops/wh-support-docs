Cúram offers different Learning Paths as a way for you to learn how to perform a certain job role, or acquire the knowledge for a particular skillset.

Our Learning Paths are comprised of courses (listed on the <Link to="/spm/education/training_courses">Available Courses</Link> page) that you take in a specific order, so that you build up the knowledge in an appropriate order and don't miss out on something that underpins a more advanced topic.

To review full details of the paths and courses contained, please download [Cúram_TrainingPaths_Courses.pdf](/pdf/education/Cúram_TrainingPaths_Courses.pdf)
